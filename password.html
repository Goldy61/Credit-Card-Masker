Password Strength Meter Demo

Warning: This is a client-side demo for educational purposes only. The strength meter evaluates passwords based on common rules (length, character variety). It does not encrypt or store passwords—use secure hashing (e.g., bcrypt) server-side in production. Test with passwords like "weak" (weak), "Medium1!" (medium), "StrongPass123!" (strong).

Strength Criteria:

  • At least 8 characters
  • At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least one number (0-9)
  • At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
